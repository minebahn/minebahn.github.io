Your Data

We collect information from you using this form to assess you and contact you regarding your builder application. If your application is unsuccessful, we will destroy the data within one month of the application's rejection. If your application is successful, we will keep the data as long as you still hold the position as a builder so we can contact you for any reason. This data is not publicly available or disclosed to anyone but Minebahn staff. Required data includes your email or discord and your minecraft username. All other requested data is specific to the builder role and does not require any personal information unless you choose to disclose it. If you have a question about how we use your data or request to remove your data, you can contact us at minebahnmtr+questions@gmail.com. Your question may be ignored if it does not include "+questions" in the email as it may be catagorized as something else.
